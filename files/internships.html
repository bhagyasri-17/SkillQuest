💡 Pro Tips for Freshers
  • Keep your LinkedIn profile updated with projects and skills before applying
  • Apply to 10–15 internships per week — volume matters when you're starting out
  • Look for "stipend + certificate" internships to build your resume quickly
  • Check company pages directly — many don't post on job boards
  • A strong GitHub profile can substitute for work experience in tech
